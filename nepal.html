✈️ Day 1: Arrival in Kathmandu – Heritage & Culture Intro

  • Arrival: Tribhuvan International Airport
  • Visit: Swayambhunath Stupa, Thamel market
  • Dinner: Nepali Thali
  • Overnight: Hotel in Kathmandu

🏯 Day 2: Kathmandu Valley UNESCO Tour

  • Pashupatinath Temple, Boudhanath Stupa
  • Patan Durbar Square, cultural dinner & dance show
  • Overnight: Stay in Kathmandu

🏞️ Day 3: Drive to Pokhara – Lakeside Town

  • Drive: 6–7 hrs via Manakamana Temple (optional cable car)
  • Evening boating on Phewa Lake & visit Barahi Temple
  • Overnight: Hotel in Pokhara

🌄 Day 4: Pokhara Sunrise + Local Adventure

  • Sarangkot sunrise view of Annapurna & Machapuchare
  • Visit Devi’s Fall, Gupteshwor Cave, World Peace Pagoda
  • Optional: Paragliding or Ultralight flight
  • Overnight: Hotel in Pokhara

🐘 Day 5: Pokhara to Chitwan – Jungle Adventures

  • Drive to Chitwan (4–5 hrs), check-in to jungle lodge
  • Tharu village walk & sunset at Rapti River
  • Overnight: Jungle lodge stay in Chitwan

🐅 Day 6: Chitwan National Park Safari

  • Morning: Canoe ride, jungle walk, elephant breeding center
  • Afternoon: Jeep Safari inside National Park
  • Overnight: Stay in Chitwan

✈️ Day 7: Return to Kathmandu – Souvenirs & Departure

  • Drive or fly back to Kathmandu
  • Visit Asan Bazaar for tea, crafts, pashmina
  • Departure: Drop to airport for return flight

🧳 Travel Tips

  • Best Time: March–May (flowers), Sep–Nov (festivals & views)
  • Carry: Warm clothes, sunglasses, power bank, ID
  • Permits: Chitwan & some treks need permits (arranged by tour)
  • Try Local: Dal Bhat, Momos, Sel Roti, Nepali tea
